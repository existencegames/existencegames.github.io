Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Existence Games ("we", "us", "our") operates the website existencegames.github.io and associated social media accounts. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Usage Data: Analytics about how visitors interact with our content across platforms (page views, engagement metrics, referral sources).
  • Platform Data: When you interact with our content on third-party platforms (Pinterest, Instagram, etc.), those platforms may provide us with aggregated analytics data.
  • Contact Information: If you voluntarily contact us, we may collect your name and email address.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Improve our content and art galleries
  • Understand audience preferences and engagement patterns
  • Manage our social media presence and posting schedule
  • Respond to inquiries

4. Third-Party Services

Our content is distributed through third-party platforms including Pinterest, Instagram, Facebook, ArtStation, DeviantArt, Redbubble, Society6, Etsy, and Printful. Each platform has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data. We encourage you to review their respective policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information for analytics purposes.

6. Cookies

This website (existencegames.github.io) does not use cookies or tracking technologies. Third-party platforms where our content appears may use their own cookies and tracking mechanisms.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect any information we collect. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us through our social media channels or by visiting our TikTok page.
